IndiGo Airlines, officially known as InterGlobe Aviation Ltd, is a prominent low-cost airline based in Gurgaon, Haryana, India. British Airways

British Airways (BA) is the United Kingdom's flag carrier airline, headquartered in London. Established in 1974 following the merger of British Overseas Airways Corporation (BOAC) and British European Airways (BEA), it is one of the world's leading airlines, known for its extensive global network, high standards of service, and strong brand reputation.

Emirates Airlines

Emirates Aviation (Arabic: طَيَران الإمارات DMG: Ṭayarān Al-Imārāt) is one of the two flag carriers of the United Arab Emirates (the other being Etihad Airways). Based in Garhoud, Dubai, the airline is a subsidiary of The Emirates Group, which is owned by the government of Dubai's Investment Corporation of Dubai. It is the largest airline in the Middle East, operating over 3,600 flights per week from its hub at Terminal 3 of Dubai International Airport.
